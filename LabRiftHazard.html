LabRift Hazard

Can you handle the mutation?

Role: Game Designer

Game Type: Game Jam

I stumbled onto this game jam when I was looking for cool projects to make and I do not regret it. It was a small team of 3 and I was responsible for designing and balancing the game, we had an artist/ programmer and a sound composer. The game feel for this game is something I’m proud of (even though it took a bit to get right).


I was also responsible for the upgrade concepts and the exp system for this game. Balancing how slowly the player gains power/ upgrades was a cool experience that I enjoy doing, but don’t necessarily get the chance to.

Sandwich Sim

Prepare a life altering sandwich

Role: Game Designer

Game Type: Quarter Game

I joined this project to help out my friends since they didn’t have a dedicated designer for the game. Everyone only had one or 2 tasks and my task was to make the layout of the kitchen and its contents. We went through 2 iterations of kitchen layouts and finally, we picked what is seen in the game.

I had a lot of fun making this game since it was pure chaos and it turned out better than I thought! (we also allowed the player to play a different game, within our game which I thought was wild and hilarious)

Here's the Link If you'd like to play the game for yourself. We have a speedrunning leaderboard!

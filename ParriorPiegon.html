Parrior Pigeon

Can you learn to parry?

Role: Level Designer

Role: Sound Designer and intro composer

Game Type: Game Jam

This was one of the most interesting game jams I’ve ever run. In an effort to learn something new, I managed to pick up sound design and a bit of music theory 24 hours before the jam and then proceeded to make the majority of the sfx and the intro music. (Granted my friend Jadon polished up everything in fmod. He’s awesome with audio and was the person teaching me everything for the jam. Shout out to Jadon, your awesome!)

I also designed 5 of the levels in the game. Overall, it was a new experience that I am so glad to have had the opportunity to have.

That Time I was an Isekai Truck and Sent People to Another World!

Send the champions other worlds need. Everyone wants to be the protagonist of an Isekai but no one wants to do the Isekaing

Role: Level Designer

Game Type: Game Jam

We started making this game during GMTK Game Jam 2023. Making the map and filling in the map wasn’t the most fun but we ended up having an amazing game with a lot of game juice. The theme of the jam was ‘Role Reversal’ and we just took that and ran with it. I started designing this map on paper, and it took me about 5 iterations to get the map where the team would like it to be. After that, it was off to unity where I implemented it along with its colliders.

The game also has a scoring system and multiplier similar to UltraKill (which we were inspired by to make the multiplier)

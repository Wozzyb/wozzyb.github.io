Root of All Evil

The trees want their vengence, the roots ask for what they deserve.

Role: Game Designer

Role: Boss Designer

Game Type: Game Jam

This was one of the first projects I’ve ever worked on and It helped me set in stone my design style as well as how I interact with others in a project. I helped prototype the concept of each attack, the map layout, as well as the win-and-lose mechanics. Dashing was a quirk that showed up in the middle of programming and we loved it so we kept it in.

Ultimately I hold this project dear to me since It was one of my first ever projects that I collaborated on. Working with people experienced in unity was a blast and I learned a lot of game design tips off of it. (It also started out my small collection of Game Design Documents).

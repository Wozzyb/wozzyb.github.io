Five Nights At Peter's

Can you survive the night?

Role: Level Designer

Game Type: Quarter Game

This game was one of the games I had the most fun in. Making the map layout and designing the enemies for horror games was an experience that helped shape my design experience. We made this game based on the popular franchise 5 Nights at Freddy’s but we added our school's mascot as the main animatronic. Alongside this, we added the zot bots, a set of robots that deliver food to students across our campus. I also helped fill out the map and designed some of the shelves and furniture on the map.

Working with 3d modelers and programmers to make this game was a blast and I learned a lot about map design and encounter designs.
